PEAS

PEAS (Peacebuilding through Education, Arts, and Sports) is an organization committed to a path that facilitates the establishment of durable peace and tries to prevent the recurrence of violence by addressing root causes and effects of conflict through Education, Arts, and Sports focusing on reconciliation, institution-building and economic transformation.

about us
what we do

PEAS (Peacebuilding through Education, Arts, and Sports)
We are dedicated to a long-term process of encouraging people to talk, repair relationships and reform institutions through contact, cooperation, communication and conciliation

Vision

Peacebuilding through Education, Arts, and Sports to create an aware and harmonious society.

Mission

Discover and address the underlying causes of conflict, and use Education, Arts and Sports as key components and mediums for peacebuilding

What we believe in

We believe in the goodness of people and understand that their quest for a
peaceful and harmonious life will bring them together through Education, Arts and Sports

We embrace the culture of continuous learning and have realized that how we work is as important as what we work on.

We recognize that not everyone starts from the same place and it is important to overcome intentional and unintentional barriers to adjust imbalances

We respect diversity and value the perspectives and contributions of all people and communities in making a positive change

We fully take responsibility for our actions, including the obligation to report, justify, and be answerable for resulting consequences to the people we work with and for.

We are committed to supporting individuals by providing them with the tools, knowledge, and resources needed to have power and control over their own live

We aim not only for immediate impact but also for long-term positive change that preserves resources for future generations

We understand that the amount of contact an individual or group has with each other could impact the peace-making process

We encourage open and transparent communication with stakeholders: supporters, volunteers, donors, beneficiaries, and the wider community to build trus

We are open to partnering with governments, local communities, businesses, and other stakeholders to amplify the impact of our initiative

We are committed to resolving disputes, repairing relationships, & reforming institutions Through the medium of Education, Arts and Sports.
